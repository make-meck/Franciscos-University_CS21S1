Sports

Franciscos University athletes excel in a variety of sports, developing teamwork, discipline, and school spirit both on and off the field.

Our students participate in basketball, football, swimming, and many other sports, representing Franciscos University in competitions and tournaments. Sports activities enhance physical fitness, leadership skills, and camaraderie among students.

We provide training, facilities, and support for our athletes to ensure they can perform at their best while maintaining academic excellence. Our sports programs cultivate teamwork, dedication, and school pride.
