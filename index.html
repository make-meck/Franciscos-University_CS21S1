CAMPUS LIFE

Franciscos University accommodates 15,000 students enrolled in various disciplines of arts and sciences. Each of which is eager to learn and improve their foundational talents, and build memorable connections with their fellow learners.

Each day at the university serves as a new learning opportunity to students. They are continuously introduced to new learning concepts with materials provided by their accommodating professors. Their knowledge is regularly tested and observed through quizzes, assessments, and other forms of class activity. During vacants, students maximize their time to prepare for their next class while also connecting with their friends.

During examination week, the atmophere is occupied by determination and drive. The school’s library, open learning spaces, and nearby coffee shops are filled with numerous diligent students putting the greatest effort to pass their exams. Once the exams are over, the intense atmosphere and the students’ exam distress subsides.

The university is not only about developing students’ academic excellence. It also provides extracurricular activities to brighten up the Franciscosian life. Every year, representatives of different organizations host a lively event where students are enthusiastically invited to join clubs that align to their interests.

Students are the topmost priority at Franciscos University. The university assures that students are effectively attaining academic excellence while living their dream university life.
