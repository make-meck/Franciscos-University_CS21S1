The History of
the University

Founded in 1975, Franciscos University started as a small institute dedicated in providing quality education in arts and science. Over the decades, it has grown into one of the leading institution with over 15, 000 students, offering diverse programs from engineering to education

The campus has expanded to include modern laboratories, libraries, sports facilities, and innovation centers. we continue to uphold our mission of excellence, research, and community engagement.
